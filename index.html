About Xiaoyang Technology

Beijing Xiaoyang Technology Co., Ltd. is an innovative technology big data enterprise dedicated to acquiring physical and mental health data through biological sensing and recognition technology.

Xiaoyang Technology integrates psychology, physiology, photogrammetry, and computer science to create a physical and mental health big data platform through AI and 3D deep neural network technologies. With advanced physical and mental health measurement technology and AI cloud services, the company provides users with convenient and low-cost health detection and management solutions.

Our mission is to make health management more intelligent and personalized through technological innovation, helping to realize a healthy life for all. Xiaoyang Technology has launched a variety of innovative products, including AI Vitality Mirror, Vitality Treasure physical and mental health measurement cloud service, which are widely used in families, enterprises and medical institutions.

With professional R&D background and technical advantages in the field of physical and mental health, Xiaoyang Technology is leading the new trend of health technology and providing more accurate and convenient health services for global users.
