About xymind.ai Technology 关于小阳科技

Beijing xymind.ai Technology Co., Ltd. is an innovative technology big data enterprise dedicated to acquiring physical and mental health data through biological sensing and recognition technology. 北京小阳科技有限公司是一家科技创新型大数据企业，致力于通过生物感知和识别技术获取身心健康数据。

xymind.ai Technology integrates psychology, physiology, photogrammetry, and computer science to create a physical and mental health big data platform through AI and 3D deep neural network technologies.
